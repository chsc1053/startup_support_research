The work was divided into the following modules:
TEAM-1
1. Document extraction from Scopus using Scopus API.
	Input: Keyword
	Output: CSV containing documents related to Input.
2. Identification of Keywords from the Scopus extracts.
	Input: Output of 1
	Output: Keywords
3. Clustering the identified keywords.
	Input: Output of 2
	Output: Clusters
TEAM-2
4. Extraction of documents related to clusters from Scopus using Scopus API.
	Input: Output of 3
	Output: CSV containing documents related to Input.
5. Summarization of extracted documents.
	Input: Output of 4
	Output: Summarized text
